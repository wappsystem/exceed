ExCEED Study

E-buddy supported Digital Sleep Therapy for
Older Adults with Cognitive Impairment.

You are invited to take part in an online research study using a new smartphone application that aims to improve your sleep and cognitive function.

SleepFix is a smartphone mobile application designed to treat insomnia using Sleep Consolidation Therapy (SCT).

SCT works to improve the balance between actual sleep time and the total amount of time spent in bed including time spent awake and asleep.

This study is for individuals who

have sleep problems
width self-reported
cognitive impairment
are over the age of 60
have access to a
smartphone

Screening Questionnaires

To determine if you are eligible to participate, you will be asked to complete a short series of screening questionnaires (max. 10-15 minutes).
You will know right away if you are eligible for the study.
