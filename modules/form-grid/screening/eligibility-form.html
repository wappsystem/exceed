ExCEED Screening Question

1. Do you sometimes fall asleep in the daytime completely without warning?

Is it literally impossible to resist ‘sleep attacks’ during the day?

Do you have collapses or extreme muscle weakness triggered by extreme emotion?

Do you have visual hallucinations, either just as you fall asleep or when you wake in the morning?

Are you paralyzed and unable to move when you wake up from your sleep?

2. Are you a very heavy snorer?

Does your partner say that you sometimes stop breathing?

Do you often wake up gasping for breath?

Are you often excessively sleepy during the day or fall asleep without wanting to?

3. Do your legs often twitch or jerk or can’t keep still in bed?

Is it very difficult to get to sleep because of repeated muscle jerks?

Do you frequently wake from sleep with sudden jerky movements or with a compulsion to move your legs?

Do you simply have to get out of bed and pace around to get rid of these feelings?

4. Do you tend to sleep well but just at the ‘‘wrong times’’?

Can you sleep well enough, but only if you stay up very late?

Are you in a very sound sleep at normal waking time and could sleep on for hours more?

Can you sleep well enough, but only if you go to bed very early?

Do you wake very early, bright and alert and no longer sleepy?

5. Do you have unusual behaviours associated with your sleep that trouble you or that are dangerous?

Do you sleepwalk frequently and run the risk of injuring yourself or others?

Do you have frequent night terrors when you are extremely distressed but not properly awake?

Do you act out your dreams and risk injuring yourself or others?

Do you have terrible recurring nightmares?
